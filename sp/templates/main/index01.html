ABOUT SCHOOLPOST

SCHOOLPOST is a virtual bank for teachers and students where they can keep track of tasks and points that the student must complete to pass the tasks. As well as the students can apply for tasks and earn points when they complete the tasks given. The student will learn how to use a virtual bank as well as how to manage money, save money, and spend responsibly using our web-based system.
